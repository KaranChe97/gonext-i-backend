  4. Correspond with Doorcart for customer support;

  6. Report problems for troubleshooting.
  7. If you sign up to use our Services as a merchant, we may collect call and SMS details.

Information we collect about you - Concerning each of your visits to the Doorcart Platform, we will automatically collect and analyze the following demographic and other information:

  1. When you communicate with us (phone, through the Doorcart Platform or otherwise), we may maintain a record of your communication;
  2. Transaction Information: We collect transaction details related to your use of our Services, and information about your activity on the Services.
  3. Stored information and files: Doorcart mobile application (Doorcart app) may also access metadata and other information associated with other files stored on your mobile device. This may include, for example, photographs.

Information we receive from other sources -

If you are a merchant partner, we may, additionally, receive feedback and rating from other users.
